TANDEM FLIGHTS

The Canary Island offers optimal weather conditions for a tandem paragliding flight. Viewed from land Lanzarote's bio diversity is impressive. With black-lava mountains, lush vegetation, white villages, and pristine coral reefs and beaches. Experienced from the air its an awe inspiring and unforgettable adventure. Like a bird, you will glide on the Laminar Sea coastal winds. Cruising over 15 km long beaches of Riscos de Famara and 600 meters above the lava island cliffs. Certain conditions can even accelerate flight over the gorgeous neighboring island La Graciosa. Wind directions can also grant flight to Orzola or the Mirador del Rio. Here you can land in paradise, relax on the beach and rejuvenate your soul in the crystal clear ocean waters.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

DO YOU HAVE ANY DOUBT?

Is there an age limit?

We take children from the age of 5 with us. There are no greater age limits!
The weight range is between 25-115 kg (55-253 lbs)

Are there any health restrictions?

You must inform us at the time of registration and prior to flight whether you have any health restrictions or complaints. We must be made aware of pregnancy, old injuries (fractures, prostheses) and hearing impairments. In this way, the pilot can adjust the take-off (or jump-start by a third party if necessary) and the landing.

What do I have to wear when flying?

Please bring sturdy shoes and warm clothes. If the local temperatures fill up well for you, we will simply pack your clothes comfortably.
