Measures to be taken into account due to the Covid19

All paragliding equipment (glider, harness, helmet) has been disinfected with approved products. When flying a tandem hang glider, a mask must be worn, and the mask must be brought directly from the client's home or vacation spot. Instructors must also be protected with masks and gloves. The flying equipment used by the clients will be disinfected after each use (harness, helmet)

LavaFly Lanzarote vehicles have been properly disinfected with approved products. If a ZenithAventura vehicle is used, we will respect the occupation recommended by the authorities and the wearing of a mask will be mandatory. Disinfectant gel is available in our vehicles and on our installations. Whenever possible, during the activity, we will establish a distance of 2 meters between each person. A waste paper basket is available on the runway and in our vehicles for the used protection material.

For your safety, and that of everyone else, it is important that you check your temperature and the existence of symptoms characteristic of Covid-19 before starting the trip.

Please pay attention to the main symptoms:

- Fever
- Fatigue
- Dry cough
- Pain or discomfort
- Nasal congestion or throat pain

In all cases, the activity will not be permitted to the person, or persons in the group coming to ZenithAventura who:

- Presents or has presented symptoms less than 14 days old
- Has been in contact with people with symptoms or who have been infected with Covid-19 before coming
- If we detect symptoms in a person in the group

In the event that we detect a client with symptoms similar to those caused by Covid-19, we will proceed to isolate the client and call 061 to receive specific instructions from the health department.
